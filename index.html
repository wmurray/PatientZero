What's the Point?

The goal of the game is to eat as many brains as possible without getting shot in the face. The first player to 13 brains wins.

Cool! How Does It Work?

Gameplay begins with Player 1. Click "Feed" button to "roll" 3 dice. The results will be one of three images:

  • 1. Brain (yay!)
  • 2. Shotgun Blast (boo!)
  • 3. Footprints (aww!)
Brains are good. Each brain scores you one point. Footprints are runners -you were too slow to catch them. They have no effect on your score. Shotguns hurt. Bad. So much so that if you get shot 3 times in a turn, you lose your turn and any points accumulated during that turn.

You can choose to stop at any time and bank your points so you do not lose them by pressing the "Rest" button.
