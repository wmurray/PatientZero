What's the Point?

The goal of the game is to eat as many brains as possible without getting shot in the face.

Cool! How Does It Work?

Gameplay begins with Player 1. Click "Feed" button to "roll" 3 dice. The results will be one of three images:

  • 1. Brain (yay!)
  • 2. Shotgun (boo!)
  • 3. Feet (aww!)

You rolled !

Based lovingly on ZombieDice by Steve Jackson, available here.
